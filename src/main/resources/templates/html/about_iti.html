PURPOSE

WD&CE's Information Technology Institute has a wide variety of noncredit, hands-on computer training classes ranging from very basic computer skills to advanced computer certification classes. Classes are offered in Microsoft Office Skills, Business Applications, Graphics and Publishing, Web Internet and Social Media, Programming Languages, Relational Databases, CISCO, Cyber Security, Microsoft Networking Certification classes and more.
