Hiring in Tech

Montgomery College

Basic Information

    Hiring in Tech is a new U.S. Department of Labor grant-funded training program for those interested in careers in computers and Information Technology (IT). Based on your skills, experience, and English level, you may be able to enter one or more tracks that will help you further your education and training and find a job.

  • PROGRAM TITLE:Hiring In Tech, Information Technology Training Program
  • ADDRESS: 51 Mannakee Street, Rockville, MD 20850
  • TERM: Varies
  • CONTACT:techhire@montgomerycollege.edu
  • PREREQUISITES:Must attend an orientation session
  • REQUIREMENTS:
    • English Language Learner
    • Unemployed with barriers to employment
    • Underemployment with barriers to better employment
  • ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS:
    • Be comfortable using computers for everyday purposes
    • Have a strong interest in an IT career
    • Have a high school diploma or GEDÂ®
    • Be legally authorized to work in the U.S.
    • Be able to increase your income or job stability through the program.
  • PROGRAM TRACKS:
    • Intensive Coaching:
      This is for people with IT skills and experience, a high English level, and who are ready to look for a job right away.
    • Short-Term Training:
      This is for people who need more help with English and IT skills and who are ready to complete classes that will last from up to 6 months. These classes will help you earn certificates to start or advance a career in the IT field.
    • Long-Term Training:
      This is for people who have a high level of English and basic skills and are ready to enter credit classes for longer training at the College, or for those who are already enrolled in those classes.
